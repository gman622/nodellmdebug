# Things humans are better at than AI

1. **Debugging** — humans use intuition and interactive exploration; AI has to guess from static code or read entire traces sequentially
2. **Web research** — humans visually scan a page and jump to the relevant result instantly; AI reads every result description linearly
3. **Filtering noise** — humans are pattern-matching scanners that instantly spot what's wrong; AI has to process everything to find the signal
